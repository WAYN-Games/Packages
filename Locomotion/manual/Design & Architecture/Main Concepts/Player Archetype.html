    Player Archetype

    This diagram shows the different components of a Player entity, and it's GameObject counterpart that handles MonoBehaviour interactions.

    erDiagram
    direction RL

    CameraControls["CameraControls : IComponentData"] {
        UnityObjectRef~Camera~ Camera "A reference to Camera MonoBehaviour on the Player GameObject (or in its children).."
        UnityObjectRef~Cinemachine Brain~ Brain "A reference to Cinemachine Brain MonoBehaviour on the Player GameObject (or in its children)."
        UnityObjectRef~Transform~ CameraTarget "A reference to the Transform of the CameraTargetAuthoring MonoBehaviour on the Player GameObject (or in its children)."
    }



    PlayerGO["Player (GameObject)"]{
        MonoBehaviour Camera "The Camera in charge of rendering the POV of the player."
        MonoBehaviour CinemachineBrain "The CinemachineBrain in charge of updating the POV of the player through Cinemachine Cameras."
        MonoBehaviour CameraTargetAuhtoring "The game object used as tracking target from the Cinemachine Cameras."
        MonoBehaviour PlayerAuthoring "The component to  author the input controls of a player."
        MonoBehaviour PlayerInput "The Player Input component that capture device inputs for read by the LocomotionInputReaderSystem."
    }

    EntityGameObject["EntityGameObject : MonoBehaviour"]{
        Entity Entity "A reference to the Player Entity."
        World World "A reference to the World the player entity lives in."
    }
